About Me

I am a 17-year-old student in London, currently studying Computer Science, Mathematics and Physics at A-level. My passion for technology and computer science began with exposure to the subject through my school education and the people around me, showing immense interest in the complexities of computing.

I love challenging myself, constantly improving my skills and striving to learn something new. My ultimate goal is to become a software engineer, specializing in frontend development, but I hope to gain experience in a multitude of fields. I am excited about the opportunity to apply my passion for programming in the workplace and be a part of shaping the future with technology.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also actively involved in online programming help communities and freelance development. I am always eager to get feedback and suggestions, and welcome any opportunity to improve my skills and knowledge in the field of Computer Science.
